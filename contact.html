Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?
We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Press and media inquiries may take slightly longer.
Can I submit my music for consideration?
Yes! We're always looking for new talent. Please send your music to submissions@musicuniverse.com with a brief bio and links to your work.
Do you offer internships or job opportunities?
We periodically offer internships and job openings. Check our Careers page or follow us on social media for announcements.
How can I report a technical issue on the website?
Please use the "Technical Support" option in the contact form above or email support@musicuniverse.com with details and screenshots if possible.
Are you available for interviews or speaking engagements?
Our team members are occasionally available for interviews and speaking engagements. Please contact press@musicuniverse.com with details about your request.
