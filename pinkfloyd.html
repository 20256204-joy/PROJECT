Band Members

Pink Floyd is an English rock band formed in London in 1965. The band is known for its progressive rock sound, philosophical lyrics, and groundbreaking live shows. Key members include Syd Barrett, Roger Waters, David Gilmour, Richard Wright, and Nick Mason.
